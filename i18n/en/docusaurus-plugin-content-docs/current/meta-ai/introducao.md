## <Icon name="PlugZap" size="md" /> Integration with Meta AI via Z-API

Meta AI offers a range of advanced functionalities that transform WhatsApp into a true digital assistant, aiming to make the user's daily life more practical and interactive. With Meta AI, users can perform searches directly within the app without needing to open browsers and receive personalized suggestions for actions and events, such as restaurant recommendations, travel locations, and even activity planning based on specified preferences and needs.

One of the features is the **"Imagine"** functionality, which allows creating images from textual descriptions provided by the user, integrating a unique visual element into interactions. This approach expands the possibilities of customer service and creativity, offering support and solutions with quick and engaging responses.

Now, the Z-API enables businesses and developers to automate conversations, respond intelligently, and significantly improve their customers' experience. This connectivity allows for more dynamic, intuitive, and effective user interactions, adding value to both support and overall customer engagement.

:::warning Important
This feature is available **only for personal WhatsApp accounts**, meaning it is not accessible for business accounts.
:::

## <Icon name="BookOpen" size="md" /> Important Concepts

### <Icon name="Cpu" size="sm" /> Meta AI

Meta AI is an artificial intelligence assistant developed by Meta that can be integrated into WhatsApp. Use this feature to create advanced chatbots, virtual assistants, and intelligent conversation experiences.

### <Icon name="Image" size="sm" /> Imagine Functionality

The **Imagine** functionality allows creating images from textual descriptions. Just send a message describing the desired image, and Meta AI will generate a corresponding image.

### <Icon name="MousePointerClick" size="sm" /> Use Cases

Meta AI can be used for:

- <Icon name="UserCheck" size="xs" /> **Create virtual assistants** for customer service
- <Icon name="MessageSquare" size="xs" /> **Implement intelligent chatbots** for automation
- <Icon name="Reply" size="xs" /> **Provide context-based automatic responses**
- <Icon name="MousePointerClick" size="xs" /> **Create personalized interactive experiences**
- <Icon name="Search" size="xs" /> **Perform direct research** within WhatsApp
- <Icon name="Image" size="xs" /> **Generate images** through textual descriptions

:::success Diverse Use Cases
Meta AI can be applied in various scenarios, from customer service to visual content generation!

## <Icon name="AlertTriangle" size="md" /> Limitations

- <Icon name="Text" size="sm" /> **Only text messages**: Audio, documents, images, and other multimedia files are not supported
- <Icon name="UserCheck" size="sm" /> **Personal accounts only**: Not available for business accounts
- <Icon name="Phone" size="sm" /> **Specific number**: Use the number `13135550002` to interact with Meta AI

:::warning Important Limitations
Remember: only text messages are supported and the feature is available only for personal accounts!
:::
