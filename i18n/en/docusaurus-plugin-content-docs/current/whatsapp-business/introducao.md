# <Icon name="Briefcase" size="lg" /> WhatsApp Business

Use specific features of the Z-API to leverage WhatsApp Business functionalities. Manage products, catalogs, tags, and notes to optimize your commercial operations.

:::tip Advanced Features
WhatsApp Business features allow you to create a complete commercial experience, from product catalogs to organizing conversations with tags!
:::

---

## <Icon name="Info" size="md" /> Overview

The WhatsApp Business section provides access to advanced features available only for verified WhatsApp Business accounts. Use these operations to manage your product catalog, organize conversations with tags, and maintain notes on customers.

---

## <Icon name="ListChecks" size="md" /> Main Operations

Manage your commercial operations with these features:

- <Icon name="Package" size="xs" /> [Products](/docs/whatsapp-business/criar-produto) - Create and manage products in your catalog
- <Icon name="Folders" size="xs" /> [Catalog](/docs/whatsapp-business/configuracao-catalogo) - Configure and manage your product catalog
- <Icon name="Tag" size="xs" /> [Tags](/docs/whatsapp-business/criar-etiqueta) - Create and manage tags to organize conversations
- <Icon name="StickyNote" size="xs" /> [Notes](/docs/whatsapp-business/atribuir-anotacoes) - Add notes to conversations and contacts
- <Icon name="FileText" size="xs" /> [Account Data](/docs/whatsapp-business/dados-conta) - Get information about your Business account

## <Icon name="CheckSquare" size="md" /> Prerequisites

To use WhatsApp Business functionalities, you need:

- <Icon name="ShieldCheck" size="sm" /> A verified WhatsApp Business account
- <Icon name="Settings" size="sm" /> Configure the catalog in WhatsApp Business Manager
- <Icon name="Package" size="sm" /> Products registered in your catalog

:::warning Required Business Account
All functionalities in this section require a verified WhatsApp Business account. Make sure your account is set up before using these APIs!
:::

---

## <Icon name="BookOpen" size="md" /> Important Concepts

### <Icon name="Package" size="sm" /> Products

Products are items you sell and can be shared through messages. Each product contains information such as name, description, price, and image.

### <Icon name="Folders" size="sm" /> Catalog

The catalog is a collection of products organized into categories. Use the catalog to present your products directly on WhatsApp.

### <Icon name="Tag" size="sm" /> Tags

Tags help organize and categorize conversations. Use tags to mark conversations by customer type, order status, or any other useful classification for your business.

:::tip Efficient Organization
Use tags to create a personalized organization system that facilitates the management of conversations and customers!
:::
