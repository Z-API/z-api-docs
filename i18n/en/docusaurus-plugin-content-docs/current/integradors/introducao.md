## <Icon name="BookOpen" size="md" /> Important Concepts

### <Icon name="Puzzle" size="sm" /> What Are Integrators?

Integrators are third-party solutions that offer interfaces, libraries, or platforms to facilitate the use of the Z-API. These solutions may include SDKs, plugins, automation platforms, and management tools.

### <Icon name="CheckSquare" size="sm" /> Compatibility

When choosing an integrator, verify its compatibility with your technology stack and the resources you need. Most integrators provide specific documentation on how to use the Z-API through their platforms.

:::info Choose Wisely
Make sure the chosen integrator is compatible with your technology stack and offers the resources you need!
:::
