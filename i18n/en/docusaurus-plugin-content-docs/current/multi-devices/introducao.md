# <Icon name="Smartphone" size="lg" /> Introduction to Multi-Devices

Understand what the WhatsApp Multi-Devices is and how our Z-API is fully compatible with this new version.

:::tip New Version of WhatsApp
The Multi-Devices is the new version of WhatsApp that allows using up to 4 devices simultaneously, without needing to keep your phone connected!
:::

## <Icon name="PlugZap" size="md" /> But what does this "blessed" version have that is special?

It comes with a series of new features, but I will highlight 2 that we consider to be the most impactful for those who use our API:

### <Icon name="Smartphone" size="sm" /> 4 Simultaneous Devices

You can have up to **four devices connected** at once, but only one phone can be linked to your WhatsApp account. In other words, you can have a mobile device and up to 3 WhatsApp Web or Desktop open simultaneously.

### <Icon name="Power" size="sm" /> Send and Receive Messages Without the Need for Your Phone to Be On

You **no longer need your phone to be on** to send and receive messages via WhatsApp Web or Desktop. However, your additional devices will be disconnected if you do not use WhatsApp on your phone for more than 14 days.

:::success Main Advantages
The two main advantages are: support for multiple simultaneous devices and independence of the phone being on!
:::

## <Icon name="AlertTriangle" size="md" /> Limitations

Are you curious to know about the limitations?

Perhaps it's not really about limitations, but rather features that are still not compatible. Below I will list the main ones:

- <Icon name="MapPin" size="xs" /> Real-time location tracking on additional devices
- <Icon name="Pin" size="xs" /> Pinning conversations in WhatsApp Web and WhatsApp for computer
- <Icon name="Users" size="xs" /> Joining groups and setting or redefining group invites in WhatsApp Web and WhatsApp for computer (you will need to use your phone to perform these actions)
- <Icon name="MessageSquare" size="xs" /> Sending messages or making calls using a connected device for contacts that use very old versions of WhatsApp
- <Icon name="Phone" size="xs" /> Making calls using the Portal or WhatsApp for computer to devices connected that are not part of the beta version for multiple devices
- <Icon name="Smartphone" size="xs" /> Other WhatsApp accounts on your Portal will only work if they are participating in the beta version for multiple devices
- <Icon name="Briefcase" size="xs" /> Changing the company name or labels in WhatsApp Business using WhatsApp Web or WhatsApp for computer

## <Icon name="CircleCheck" size="md" /> Compatibility with Z-API

Is the Z-API compatible with this new version?

**Yes!** We are proud to share that we were able to make all methods compatible and abstract away all the complexity of the new version in order to deliver to our customers a simple and transparent way all features of this new version.

But calm down, the changes went far beyond the features mentioned above. There are much more things involved. For us, the most critical was the change in the encryption model that allows maintaining end-to-end security even without your phone being on.

:::success Full Compatibility
The Z-API is fully compatible with Multi-Devices! We abstracted all the complexity for you, maintaining the security and complete functionalities.
:::
