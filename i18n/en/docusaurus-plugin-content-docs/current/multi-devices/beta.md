## About the beta Z-API multi-device

We are here **"pulling our hair out"** to deliver all the new features of this new version in the most transparent way possible for you :)

But with the same transparency as always, we need to tell you that almost nothing was carried over from the previous version. We had to create a new version where little was taken from the previous one. This was very cool because it allowed us to further improve our performance and stability.

During this process, our team has been working hard to deliver the same experience as before with the benefits of this new version. Our tests are being meticulous and comprehensive, but just like every good Dev you already know that at some point we may identify a point that went unnoticed in our tests. When that happens, know that we will be here to find a solution as quickly as possible and fix it in record time.

---

## Beta Checklist

1 - Be a client with at least one active instance.

2 - Have a number with the version that allows enabling multi-devices.

3 - Be willing to contribute to the evolution of Z-API.
