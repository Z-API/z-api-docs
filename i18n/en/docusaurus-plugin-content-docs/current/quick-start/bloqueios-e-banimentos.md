# <Icon name="ShieldAlert" size="lg" /> Best Practices to Avoid Lockouts

Using automation on WhatsApp is a powerful tool, but it's essential to follow best practices to ensure the security of your account and avoid lockouts or bans.

:::warning Essential Reading
This page is an essential guide. Read carefully to understand how to use Z-API responsibly and efficiently.
:::

---

## <Icon name="Heart" size="md" /> The Golden Rule: Act Like a Human

The main cause of lockouts is behavior that WhatsApp identifies as non-human or spam. The best way to avoid problems is to make your automations behave similarly to a real person.

:::tip Pro Tip
Think of your automations as an extension of your personal communication. The more natural and contextually relevant, the better!
:::

---

## <Icon name="CircleCheck" size="md" /> What to Do (Best Practices)

- <Icon name="RefreshCw" size="sm" /> **Warm Up Your Account:** If the number is new or has little usage, start by sending a few messages per day and gradually increase the volume over several weeks. Engage in group chats and interact manually.
- <Icon name="MessageSquareText" size="sm" /> **Send Relevant Messages:** Ensure that recipients expect to receive your messages. Send valuable content, such as order confirmations, appointment reminders, or answers to questions.
- <Icon name="UserCheck" size="sm" /> **Personalize Messages:** Whenever possible, include the recipient's name or other relevant information in your messages. Avoid sending the same generic message to hundreds of contacts.
- <Icon name="LogOut" size="sm" /> **Respect Opt-Out:** Offer a clear way for users to stop receiving your messages (for example, "Reply 'STOP' to unsubscribe") and respect these requests immediately.
- <Icon name="Smartphone" size="sm" /> **Use a Dedicated Number:** If possible, use a dedicated phone number exclusively for your automations, separate from your personal or main business number.

:::success Best Practices
Following these guidelines not only protects your account but also improves the experience of your contacts and increases engagement!
:::

---

## <Icon name="Ban" size="md" /> What Not to Do (Risk Practices)

- <Icon name="XSquare" size="sm" /> **Do Not Spam:** Never send messages in bulk to people who did not request or have no prior relationship with your business. This is the fastest way to be blocked and banned.
- <Icon name="Timer" size="sm" /> **Avoid Sudden Mass Sendings:** Do not start sending hundreds or thousands of messages at once, especially from a new number. This is a big red flag for WhatsApp.
- <Icon name="Link" size="sm" /> **Do Not Send Only Links:** Messages that contain only a link, especially if it's shortened, are often flagged as suspicious. Always add context text.
- <Icon name="ShieldX" size="sm" /> **Be Careful with Sensitive Content:** Avoid sending content that could be considered illegal, misleading, or that violates WhatsApp's commercial policies.
- <Icon name="Eye" size="sm" /> **Do Not Ignore Blocks:** If many users block your number in a short period, WhatsApp will interpret this as a signal that your messages are unwanted.

:::danger Attention
These practices can result in permanent account lockout. Always prioritize quality over quantity!
:::

---

## <Icon name="ShieldAlert" size="md" /> Warning Signs to Watch For

Be alert to these signs, as they may indicate that your account is at risk:

- <Icon name="Clock" size="sm" /> **Delays in Delivery:** Your messages take much longer than normal to be delivered.
- <Icon name="XSquare" size="sm" /> **Delivery Failures:** You start seeing an increasing number of errors when trying to send messages.
- <Icon name="BellRing" size="sm" /> **Direct Warnings:** In some cases, WhatsApp may send a warning in the app.

:::warning Immediate Action
If you notice any of these signs, immediately reduce your message volume and review your sending practices!
:::

---

## <Icon name="ShieldBan" size="md" /> What to Do if Your Account is Blocked?

Even if you follow best practices, your account may still be blocked. The recovery process generally involves contacting official WhatsApp support.

For more details on how to proceed, see the documentation on **[Request Unban](/docs/mobile/solicitar-desbanimento)**.

:::info Support
The recovery process may take several days. Be patient and provide all requested information to WhatsApp support.
:::
