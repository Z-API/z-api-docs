# <Icon name="Activity" size="lg" /> Stability with Z-API

Essential tips to maintain a stable and high-quality connection with the Z-API.

---

## Introduction

For service quality, we have some tips that can greatly help in maintaining stability and quality of your connection with Z-API. Below, we will cover these points in topics.

---

## Dedicated Device

We know it's not always possible, but if you can avoid using the device for other activities, it can contribute to good stability and quality of Z-API services. A good tip is to use Android emulators. For more information, look for the topic [Emulators](/docs/tips/emuladores) in this same section.

---

## Device Always Connected

For Z-API to function, the device needs to be connected and always online. Just like with WhatsWeb, if the device loses connection, our service stops working. Therefore, it is very important that it remains always connected to the internet.

---

## Good Internet Connection

Keep your phone connected to a Wi-Fi network with good speed and avoid using only the mobile data plan. The speed of your internet directly impacts the quality of information exchange between your phone and Z-API.

---

## WhatsApp Always Open

Keeping the WhatsApp app open helps maintain the connection between our service and your phone, just like with WhatsWeb, we need to communicate with your phone. When you lock or block your phone, WhatsApp may stop running in the background, causing a loss of internet connection, which will result in unavailability of our service. Finally, if possible, we recommend installing the Keep Phone On app, which helps keep your device always on.

---

## Summary of Good Practices

- Use dedicated device or emulator
- Keep device always connected to the internet
- Use good quality Wi-Fi connection
- Keep WhatsApp always open
- Avoid locking the screen of the device
- Use apps to keep device active
