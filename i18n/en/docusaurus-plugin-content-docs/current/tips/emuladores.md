# <Icon name="Monitor" size="lg" /> Android Emulators

Recommendations for Android emulators to improve the quality and stability of the Z-API service.

---

## Introduction

To really have a high-quality service running smoothly, we recommend using Android emulators if possible. While it's not a requirement, we know that utilizing this feature can greatly enhance the quality of the service. There are many solutions available in the market, including cloud services prepared to provide hosted phone numbers as a service. Below, we will list some solutions where our customers have successfully operated.

---

## Recommended Emulators

- **[BlueStacks](https://www.bluestacks.com/pt-br/bluestacks-5.html)**
- **[Nox](https://nox-app-player.br.uptodown.com/windows)**
- **[Memu](https://www.memuplay.com)**
- **[LDPlayer](https://pt.ldplayer.net/ldy/pt2071.html)**
- **[Cloud Emulator](https://www.cloudemulator.net)**

But feel free to look for other options.

---

## VoIP Numbers

To add the final touch, you can still sync the emulator with a VoIP number. To learn how to activate it, read the topic [Enable Fixed Number](/docs/tips/habilitar-numero-fixo) in this same section.

---

## Benefits

- **Better Stability**: Dedicated emulators offer better performance
- **Availability**: Does not depend on a physical device
- **Scalability**: Easy to scale and manage multiple instances
- **Backup**: Easy to make backups and restore configurations
