# <Icon name="Phone" size="lg" /> Enable Fixed Number

Step-by-step guide to enable a fixed number in WhatsApp Business.

---

## Introduction

To enable a fixed number, you will need a mobile phone to host your fixed number. (this option is available in WhatsApp Business). The step-by-step we created uses an Android phone.

---

## First Step

Download the WhatsApp Business app on a mobile phone:

- **[Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.whatsapp.w4b&hl=pt_BR&gl=US)**
- **[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/whatsapp-business/id1386412985)**

![WhatsApp Business](/img/WAB.jpeg)

---

## Second Step

The WhatsApp Business app will ask for permission to access the phone's files, choose continue to grant access. On the next screen, select your country and enter your fixed number.

![WhatsApp Business Terms](/img/WABTermos.png)

---

## Third Step

You will receive a code, but since it's not possible to receive an SMS on a fixed number, wait until the countdown ends and choose the **Call Me** option.

And you're done! You will receive a call from WhatsApp Business that will read out the activation code. Just type it in the indicated field.

![WhatsApp Business Code](/img/WABCodigo.png)

---

## Fourth Step

Be Happy!

Set up your WhatsApp Business app and scan the Z-API QR code to unleash your imagination!
