## Introduction

In the last few weeks, messages containing buttons have been experiencing instability in their functionality.

**It is important to remember that this is not an exclusive problem of Z-API.**

This topic describes how button behavior is in different scenarios on WhatsApp.

---

:::danger Attention

This documentation was updated on 27/08/2024. The facts about button functionality are based on the reality of this day. It is important to remember that with each WhatsApp update, buttons may undergo changes.

:::

## Decisive Factors:

#### For the functioning of buttons in WhatsApp, there are two decisive factors:

- Whether the WhatsApp sending the message is business or normal
- Whether the message is being sent to a regular chat or a group.
- Accepting the terms of use for the buttons

## Acceptance of Button Terms of Use

To utilize the button functionality, it is necessary that you accept the terms of use, recognizing that you are aware that future WhatsApp updates may cause instabilities in this functionality.

![img](/img/buttons-terms.jpeg)

## Types of Buttons

#### There are seven types of buttons on WhatsApp:

- Simple text button (/send-button-list)
- Simple image button (/send-button-list-image)
- Simple video button (/send-button-list-video)
- Option list (/send-option-list)
- Action buttons (/send-button-actions)
- Copy button (/send-button-otp)
- Pix button (/send-button-pix)
