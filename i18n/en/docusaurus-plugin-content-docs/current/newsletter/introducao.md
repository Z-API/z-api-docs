# Introduction to Channels

In this topic, you will learn about all the methods available for manipulating WhatsApp Channels (Newsletters).

## Conceptualization

WhatsApp Channels are similar to Facebook pages or Telegram channels, providing a space to transmit information, news, announcements, and other related content to an unlimited number of followers.

These channels can be created by a variety of entities, from football teams to companies that offer streaming services, allowing users to follow and engage with the content they are interested in.

:::info Channel IDs

It is important to emphasize that WhatsApp's standard for channel IDs always includes the suffix **`@newsletter`**.

Example: `1234567890@newsletter`

:::

## Available Features

Through the Z-API, you can:

- Create new channels
- Update information (name, description, image)
- Follow and unfollow channels
- Mute and reactivate notifications
- Manage administrators
- Transfer ownership of a channel
- Search and list channels

Explore the next sections to learn how to use each of these features.
