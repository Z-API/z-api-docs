## Introduction

To truly have a quality service running smoothly, we recommend — where possible — using Android emulators. It's not a requirement, but we know that using this resource can greatly help service quality. There are several solutions on the market, including cloud services prepared to host your number. Below we list some solutions that our clients are successfully using.
